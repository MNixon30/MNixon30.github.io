T-Junction Mixing

1. Problem Definition
1.1 Problem

2 Fluids, water and oil are passed through a t-junction with mixing slots indented within the pipe. This simulation aims to determine how well theses substances mix over time (transient). An external shaking acceleration will also be added (for fun) using UDF's to see how shaking of the juction can effect mixing while the fluids are flowing.
